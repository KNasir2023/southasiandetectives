In my formative years, detective novels had a profound impact in my life. The detectives, although fictional characters only existed in the fictional universe, entertained thousands of teen agers across the globe. The British Empire had the most recognizable – Sherlock Holmes with Doctor John Watson. The Francophonic world had Inspector Jules Maigret. As a South Asian, I grew up reading the works of many extraordinary gifted authors, given us a number of larger than life characters. Thanks to these talented authors, South Asia had been a home for generations of sleuths. The love for these sleuths was the driving force behind this project. This is the only project to the best of my knowledge that highlights the origin, timeline, the protagonists, investigative techniques and missions that were undertaken by the indomitable detectives of South Asia. The project is made with book illustrations, maps and open data to find places described in fictions, approaches towards investigations and creating a virtual community of lovers of sleuth fictions.
